Privacy Policy

VoicePaper · Effective date: June 23, 2026

VoicePaper is built privacy-first. Your journal — your voice, your words, your photos — stays on your device by default and is processed on-device.

What we collect

  • Nothing is uploaded to us. Your recordings, transcripts, photos, and entries are stored on your device. We run no servers and never receive your journal content.
  • The app requires no sign-up and collects no account, name, email, or location.
  • We use no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Microphone & voice-to-text

Microphone access is used only to record your voice when you tap record. Speech-to-text transcription runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Speech framework — your audio is never uploaded to us or any third party.

On-device mood insights

Emotion and keyword analysis is performed locally with Apple's built-in Natural Language framework. Your entries are not sent anywhere to generate insights or AI summaries.

iCloud sync (optional, Pro)

If you turn on iCloud sync, your entries are stored in your own private iCloud account under your Apple ID, managed by Apple. We have no access to that data. Sync is off by default; without it, your journal stays only on this device. You can disable it or remove iCloud copies anytime in your device's iCloud settings.

Photos

Photos you add to an entry are stored with your journal on your device (and in your iCloud if sync is on). We do not upload them anywhere else.

Storage and deletion

You can delete any entry inside the app, or remove everything by deleting the app. If iCloud sync was enabled, manage or delete those copies in your device's iCloud settings.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the one-time Lifetime purchase are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Face ID / passcode lock

The optional Face ID lock is handled by the system. We never see or store your biometric data.

Children

VoicePaper is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@windylabs.app

隐私政策

絮语日记（VoicePaper）· 生效日期：2026 年 6 月 23 日

絮语日记把你的隐私放在第一位。你的日记——你的声音、你的文字、你的照片——默认只留在你的设备上，并在设备端完成处理。

我们收集什么

  • 不向我们上传任何内容。录音、转写文字、照片与日记条目只保存在你的设备上。我们没有任何服务器，绝不会接收你的日记内容。
  • App 不需要注册，不收集账号、姓名、邮箱或位置信息。
  • 不使用任何第三方统计或广告 SDK。

麦克风与语音转文字

麦克风权限仅用于在你点击录音时录制你的声音。语音转文字全部在你的设备上完成（使用 Apple 端侧 Speech 框架），你的音频绝不会上传给我们或任何第三方。

端侧情绪洞察

情绪与关键词分析在本机使用 Apple 内置的 Natural Language 框架完成。生成洞察或 AI 小结时，你的日记内容不会被发送到任何地方。

iCloud 同步（可选，Pro）

若你开启 iCloud 同步，你的日记会保存在你自己的、归属于你 Apple ID 的私有 iCloud 账户中，由 Apple 管理，我们无法访问这些数据。同步默认关闭；不开启时，日记只留在本机。你可以随时在设备的 iCloud 设置中关闭同步或删除 iCloud 副本。

照片

你添加到日记中的照片会随日记保存在你的设备上（若开启同步则也在你的 iCloud 中）。我们不会把它们上传到其他任何地方。

存储与删除

你可以在 App 内删除任意日记，或直接删除 App 清除全部数据。若曾开启 iCloud 同步，可在设备的 iCloud 设置中管理或删除这些副本。

购买

订阅与一次性永久买断由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理，我们不会接触或存储你的支付信息。

Face ID / 密码锁

可选的 Face ID 锁由系统处理，我们绝不会看到或存储你的生物识别数据。

儿童

絮语日记不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集他们的信息。

变更

若本政策有变更，我们会更新本页面与上方的生效日期。
