Privacy Policy

SnoreScribe · Effective date: June 10, 2026

SnoreScribe puts your privacy first. The app is designed so that your audio never leaves your device.

What we collect

  • Nothing is uploaded. Recordings and audio clips are stored only on your device and are never sent to any server.
  • Snore detection and report analysis run entirely on-device.
  • The app requires no sign-up and collects no account, name, email, or location.
  • We use no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Microphone

SnoreScribe asks for microphone access solely to record sounds while you sleep, so it can detect and analyze snoring. Recording happens only when you start a session and stops when you end it.

Storage and deletion

All data — recordings, audio clips, and reports — lives in the app's local storage on your device. You can erase everything at any time in Settings → Delete All Recordings, or by deleting the app.

Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Future features

If optional cloud or AI features are added later, we will ask for your consent separately, and they would only process anonymous numeric summaries — never the audio itself.

Not medical advice

SnoreScribe is a lifestyle logging tool. Its data and interpretations are for reference only and do not constitute a medical diagnosis, treatment advice, or screening conclusion.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@windylabs.app

SnoreScribe 把你的隐私放在第一位。产品在设计上保证：你的音频不会离开你的设备。

我们收集什么

  • 不上传任何内容。录音与音频片段只保存在你的设备本机，绝不发送到任何服务器。
  • 鼾声检测与报告分析全部在设备端完成。

麦克风

SnoreScribe 申请麦克风权限仅用于在你睡眠时录制声音，以检测和分析鼾声。只有你主动开始监听才会录音，结束后即停止。

存储与删除

全部数据（录音、音频片段、报告）都保存在设备本机。你可以随时在「设置 → 删除所有录音」中清除全部数据，或直接删除 App。

若未来加入可选的云端或 AI 功能，会单独征得你的同意，并且只处理匿名的数字摘要，不会传输音频本身。

SnoreScribe 是一款生活方式记录工具，提供的数据与解读仅供参考，不构成医疗诊断、治疗建议或筛查结论。
